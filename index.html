Getting help is easier than you think

1) TELL US YOUR REQUIREMENTS

After giving you a thorough listening, our representative would make a note of all your needs. What you want to learn, when you want to study it, the gender of the tutor, how much you can afford, etc.

2) SCHEDULE MEETINGS WITH TUTORS

We sort our tutor database based on your needs, then give the tutors your contact information. Tutor meetings can be scheduled at a time that works best for you.

3) DEMONSTRATION CLASS

You can meet with the tutor to discuss your needs, including the number of tutoring days, during a trial class.

4) MAKE PAYMENTS AND START LEARNING

Paying TEACH TRACK in advance is required. And if you pay tutors directly,Then TEACH TRACK is no longer guarantees the tutor and will not be held accountable.
